About Us

Tiba Pharmacy Store is your trusted partner in health and wellness. We provide a wide range of medications and healthcare products to ensure you and your family stay healthy. Our online platform allows you to conveniently order medicines from the comfort of your home, with fast delivery to your location. We pride ourselves on our excellent customer service, professional advice, and commitment to your well-being.

Our pharmacy is equipped with the latest technology to ensure the safety and security of your personal information. We offer a seamless and user-friendly interface for an effortless shopping experience. Our highly trained pharmacists are always available to answer any questions and provide professional guidance.

Whether you need prescription medicines, over-the-counter drugs, or healthcare products, Tiba Pharmacy Store is here to serve you. Our mission is to provide high-quality healthcare services and products at affordable prices. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, and we strive to make that a reality for our customers.

Mission and Vision Statement

‘Helping the Nation to Look and Feel Good One Person at a Time’. Our vision is to be the first-choice primary healthcare and wellness provider in Eastern Africa, reaching the lives of 5 million people by 2022.

Our Values

At Tiba Pharmacy Store, we uphold the highest ethical standards in all our operations. We are committed to providing the best healthcare services and products to our customers. We believe in integrity, professionalism, and dedication to customer satisfaction. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that you receive the best care and advice. We care deeply about the health and well-being of our community and strive to make a positive impact on the lives of our customers.
